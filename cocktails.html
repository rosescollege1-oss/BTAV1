Signature Cocktails

A curated collection of elevated cocktails designed to feel polished, memorable, and celebration-worthy.

Electric Airhead

A bold, electric blend of blueberry vodka, bright lemonade, and blue curaçao with a splash of sweet & sour for that perfect tang. Finished with a sour powder rim for a nostalgic kick that tastes just like your favorite candy.

Electric Airhead Charger

Everything you love about the Electric Airhead — amplified. Blueberry vodka, lemonade, and blue curaçao collide with sweet & sour and a splash of Red Bull for an extra burst of energy.

Signature Margarita

A refined twist on the classic. Smooth reposado tequila meets fresh lime juice and triple sec, balanced with agave for just the right touch of sweetness. Served with a salted rim and fresh lime garnish.

Spicy Margarita

Bold, vibrant, and not for the faint of heart. Reposado tequila infused with jalapeño heat is muddled fresh with sliced jalapeños, shaken with lime juice and triple sec, and finished with a Tajín rim.

Skinny Margarita

Clean, crisp, and refreshingly simple. Reposado tequila meets fresh lime juice and triple sec with no added sweetener — letting the bright citrus and smooth tequila shine through.

Strawberry Shortcake

Sweet, creamy, and just a little indulgent. Whipped vodka blends with cranberry juice for a soft berry finish, topped with whipped cream and served with a graham cracker rim and fresh strawberry garnish.

Yaeger Bomb

A high-energy classic. Sugar-free Red Bull meets chilled Jägermeister for a smooth, bold drop that keeps the celebration alive. Simple. Strong. Straight to the point.

Copper Cauldron

A rich, smooth blend of Four Roses Small Batch and Maker’s Mark bourbon, brightened with fresh lemon juice and honey syrup. Finished with a splash of root beer for a subtle caramel warmth.

Mojito

Bright, crisp, and endlessly refreshing. Fresh mint and lime are gently muddled and shaken with smooth rum, then topped with sparkling soda for a clean, citrus-forward finish.

Old Fashioned

Timeless and refined. Smooth bourbon stirred with sugar and bitters, finished with a citrus peel for subtle warmth and depth. Simple. Strong. Classic.

Moscow Mule

Vodka, fresh lime juice, and spicy ginger beer served ice-cold for a crisp, zesty finish. Clean, refreshing, and always a crowd favorite.

Aperol Spritz

Bright and bubbly with a bittersweet citrus edge. Aperol blended with prosecco and topped with soda creates a light, refreshing cocktail perfect for golden hour celebrations.

Lemon Drop

Crisp vodka shaken with fresh lemon juice and triple sec, served with a sugared rim for the perfect balance of sweet and tart. Smooth, bright, and effortlessly fun.

Blackberry Bourbon Smash

Bold bourbon muddled with fresh blackberries and lemon, lightly sweetened and shaken to perfection. Rich, slightly tart, and beautifully balanced.
